My name is Sehyun Kim (also known as ‘av’ or ‘visualozik’). I am a visual artist from Seoul, Korea.

I started my career in 2007 as a freelance motiongraphic designer and indie music video director. I co-found a visual studio ‘Visualozik’ in 2009. We started to expand our body of work into a variety of directions, that range, from 3d projection mapping, experimental film, graphic design, motion graphic, 3d animation, commercials, music video, and more.

In 2014, I moved to New York to start a new challenge. I am a graduate student at NYU’s Interactive Telecommunications Program (ITP). My focus at ITP has been creative coding and working with new platforms where digital media can be displayed in a unique way.
